Viscerally Imaginative Techno Experience

We'll set your minds to travel far into the realms of imagination!
Expect to refresh your ears with the latest tendencies in techno - we always aim for the highest sound quality, and superb production of the music we play; we put the main focus on the vibe to maximize that sonic experience.
Amsterdam based record label Consumed Music launched Imagica events in 2014 and since has produced many memorable nights for international techno lovers.
